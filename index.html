About Me

I am Xuan Li, a second-year Ph.D. student at TMLR Group of Hong Kong Baptist University, fortunate to be advised by Prof. Bo Han and co-advised by Prof. Lu (Eric) Zhang, and working with Prof. Jiangchao Yao. My research focuses on trustworthy machine reasoning for scientific discovery, aiming to turn advanced reasoning capabilities into reliable, verifiable scientific tools.

My research interests include:

  • Agentic Systems for Complex Reasoning: Methodologies for continuously improving reasoning quality : [AlphaApollo]
  • Probing Reasoning Reliability: Investigating robustness and safety in LLMs: [DeepInception]
  • Advancing Reasoning for Scientific Discovery: Reasoning for molecular property prediction and optimization: [Neural Atoms], [RePO]
